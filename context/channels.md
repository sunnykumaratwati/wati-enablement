# Channels

_Last updated: 2026-07-01 · Source: Q1 2026 & Q4 '25 quarterly decks, WhatsApp Business Calling deck_

## What It Is
Wati is multichannel — start on any channel, scale on all. Manage WhatsApp, Instagram, Messenger, RCS, SMS, TikTok Ads, and WhatsApp Calling from one platform and unified inbox.

## Positioning One-Liner
"WhatsApp, Instagram, Messenger, RCS, SMS, Voice, TikTok Ads — all in one platform. Start using Wati with any channel, scale on all."

## Sub-Features
| Feature | What it does | Plan |
|---------|-------------|------|
| WhatsApp | Core channel — messaging, broadcasts, automations | All |
| WhatsApp Business Calling | Voice calls inside WhatsApp — inbound (UIC, free) & outbound (BIC) | All (except Pay As You Go) |
| Instagram | DMs + reply to comments → start conversations | All |
| Messenger | FB Messenger in unified inbox | All |
| RCS | Google-verified rich messaging, up to 200K broadcasts, "Seen" metric | All (except PAYG) |
| SMS | SMS broadcast + fallback when WhatsApp fails | Business |
| TikTok Ads | TikTok ads that click to WhatsApp, with attribution | On request (Beta) |
| Start without a WhatsApp number | Sign up with Instagram/Messenger first, add WhatsApp later | All |

## Proof Points (real only)
- 8+ channels now live
- One of the only platforms supporting TikTok click-to-WhatsApp ads
- WhatsApp Calling: 77% believe a call is the fastest way to get an answer

## Positioning Notes / Do's & Don'ts
- WhatsApp Calling: UIC (User Initiated) is free, during business hours, best for support. BIC (Business Initiated) for outbound.
- Meta call permission expires in 7 days — the "Request Accepted" dialer view tracks who granted permission.
- RCS is carrier-backed, compliant, good for markets/geographies with lower WhatsApp adoption.
