# AI — Astra (AI Agents)

_Last updated: 2026-07-01 · Source: Wati AI Webinar Deck, Q1 2026 & Q4 '25 quarterly decks_

## What It Is
Astra is Wati's autonomous AI agent layer — your 24/7 digital workforce. Agents don't just answer questions; they complete entire business processes (qualify leads, book appointments, handle FAQs) across Web, WhatsApp, and Voice. Sold as an add-on to Wati plans.

## Positioning One-Liner
"Your 24/7 Digital Workforce — move beyond 'chat' to 'action'." External automation & revenue; benefits your customers.
