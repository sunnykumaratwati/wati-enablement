# Campaigns & Marketing

_Last updated: 2026-07-01 · Source: Wati for Marketing deck, Q1 2026 quarterly deck, Campaign Calendar launch project_

## What It Is
Wati's marketing engine turns WhatsApp into a revenue channel — broadcasts, campaign planning, drip engagement, segmentation, and click-to-WhatsApp (CTWA) ads with conversion attribution.

## Positioning One-Liner
"Conversations (not campaigns) build relationships, and relationships build revenue." Wati turns WhatsApp into your biggest revenue channel across Acquire → Engage → Convert → Retain.

## Sub-Features
| Feature | What it does | Plan |
|---------|-------------|------|
| Broadcasts | Bulk messaging with targeting & scheduling; 500+ MPS on request | All |
| Campaign Calendar | Visual weekly calendar to plan campaigns (🟢 sent 🟣 scheduled 🔵 AI-recommended) | Pro & Business |
| Wati Recommended Campaigns | AI suggests 2–5 campaigns/week tuned to business + region + festivals | Pro & Business (AI) |
| Drip Campaigns | Multi-message engagement sequence (2–5 msgs), auto-stops on reply | Every plan |
| Custom Segments | Reusable segments from any filter; auto-update or static snapshot | Pro & Business |
| Auto-launch on Meta approval | Campaign fires the instant Meta approves the template | Growth/Pro/Business |
| Campaign reply → automation | Map quick-reply buttons to actions | Growth/Pro/Business |
| CTWA Ads | Google/Meta/TikTok ads that click to WhatsApp + conversion tracking (CAPI/WCI) | Business + Ads add-on |

## Positioning Notes / Do's & Don'ts
- **Drip Campaigns is sequencing logic — NEVER call it AI.** AI claim only on Wati Recommended Campaigns.
- Campaign Calendar & Recommended Campaigns are ONE feature set; Drip is SEPARATE. Never imply Drip is nested in the Calendar.
- Drip framing = ENGAGEMENT, not "follow-up."
- Banned words: engine, orchestration, "stay top of mind", "leak/leaking pipe", Drip Sequences, Wati Recommends, Wati AI Campaigns. (Autopilot allowed.)
