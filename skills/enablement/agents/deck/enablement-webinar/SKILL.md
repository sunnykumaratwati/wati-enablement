# Launch-Enablement Webinar Deck (Internal)

**First read the shared `skills/enablement/agents/deck/DECK-PRINCIPLES.md`** — universal rules for every deck.

This agent builds the **internal enablement deck** for a launch: a hands-on, build-along session that equips Sales/CS/Support to sell and support a new feature. It is one of four deck types under `deck/` — distinct from the customer quarterly webinar, the customer product deck, and onboarding-deck slides. Its storyline is training-oriented, not a customer narrative.

## Storyline (internal enablement)
- What's launching + who it's for (plan eligibility)
- Why it matters to the customer (the value, in the rep's words)
- How to build/use it — hands-on, build-along steps
- What to pitch it against / where it wins
- Objection handling + FAQs (the questions customers ask)
- Prep ask + next steps for the team
