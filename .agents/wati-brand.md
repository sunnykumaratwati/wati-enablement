# Wati Brand Context

Use this file as the foundational brand and narrative reference for all Wati enablement assets. Every deck, brief, and one-pager should feel consistent with what's here.

---

## What Wati Is

Wati is a WhatsApp-first, AI-focused platform that helps businesses deliver outstanding customer experience at scale. It is a Business Solution Provider (BSP) built on top of the WhatsApp Business API.

Core positioning: **"We empower brands to balance agent efficiency and speed on WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and Instagram."**

---

## The Master Narrative Arc

All Wati content follows this story arc. Every deck should move through these beats — even if some are compressed.

### 1. Context / The World Today
Customers expect more. They want:
- 24/7 support — businesses that don't have it lose leads
- Real-time, asynchronous messaging — not email, not call queues
- Personalization at scale

Key stat: WhatsApp has won the war of messaging apps in key markets. But just adding WhatsApp is no longer enough.

### 2. The Problem with the Status Quo
- Agents waste time gathering context, switching tools, managing tickets manually
- Email is not real-time — customers take longer to reply, conversations stall
- Without automation, support can't scale without burning out agents

### 3. The Shift
To meet modern support demands, you need a packaged solution that:
- Gives customers the convenience of instant, asynchronous messaging on WhatsApp
- Helps you unlock support efficiency at scale with AI

### 4. The Wati Solution — 4 Pillars
Every Wati story comes back to these four pillars:

| Pillar | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **AI & Automation** | AI Support Agent resolves up to 60% of tickets automatically. Chatbots handle routine queries 24/7. |
| **Broadcasts & Proactive Messaging** | Reach customers before they reach you. Announcements, shipping updates, reminders. 30% higher CSAT form fills vs email. |
| **Unified Team Inbox** | All conversations — WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, FB Messenger — in one place. Auto-assign, collaborate, track. |
| **Human Connect** | Seamless AI-to-human handoff. The best CX comes from the right balance of automation and human touch. |

### 5. Proof
- 100+ countries served
- Resolve up to 60% of support tickets with AI
- 30% higher CSAT form fills than email
- Support resolution times drop from days to minutes (Hakeem case study)
- Trusted for reliability — one of the best delivery rates in the industry

### 6. Why Wati vs. Others
- vs other BSPs: More features, better AI, purpose-built for support + marketing
- vs cPaaS vendors: No-code setup, built for business teams not developers
- Built for complexity: custom workflows, chatbots, targeting without hand-holding

### 7. Next Steps / CTA
Always end with a clear, low-friction next step:
- Existing customers: support.wati.io / raise a ticket / book a 1-to-1 call
- New contacts: Book a demo / start a pilot

## Brand Voice

- **Direct, not corporate.** Say "resolve 60% of tickets" not "optimize resolution throughput."
- **Empowering, not feature-listing.** Lead with what the customer can do, not what the product has.
- **Conversational but professional.** Wati talks like a smart operator, not a brochure.
- **Stats anchor every claim.** Never make a benefit claim without a number or a customer story behind it.

## Visual Identity (for slide design)

- **Primary green**: `#25D366` (WhatsApp green — use as accent, not dominant)
- **Wati dark**: `#1A1A2E` or deep navy for dark slides
- **Wati brand green** (actual): use `#00B09B` as primary CTA/accent color
- **White background** for content slides; dark for covers and section breaks
- **Font**: Calibri or Arial (safe for PowerPoint rendering)
- Logo: always bottom-right on content slides, centered on cover/thank-you
